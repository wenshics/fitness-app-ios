## Full Description

### First Paragraph (Primary Keywords)
Pulse is your daily workout companion and fitness tracker designed to keep you motivated and consistent. Whether you're looking for daily exercise routines, home workouts, or strength training plans, Pulse delivers personalized fitness goals and workout plans tailored to your lifestyle. With our AI-powered personal trainer, every day brings a new opportunity to crush your fitness goals.

### Key Features
- **AI-Generated Daily Workouts**: Get fresh, personalized exercise plans every day across 30+ exercises in 3 categories (cardio, strength, flexibility)
- **Built-In Workout Timer**: Track your exercise with precision using our integrated workout timer for perfect pacing and rest intervals
- **Fitness Tracker & Progress**: Log your daily workouts, build streaks, and watch your fitness goals come to life with real-time progress tracking
- **Habit Tracker**: Stay accountable with visual streaks and habit tracking that motivates you to work out every day
- **Achievement System**: Unlock 26 unique badges and achievements as you hit milestones - from your first workout to mastering strength training
- **Smart Reminders**: Set custom reminder times for your daily exercise sessions and never miss a workout
- **Home Workouts & Gym Ready**: Whether you prefer home workouts or gym sessions, Pulse adapts to your environment
- **Personal Insights**: Track your fitness journey with stats on workouts completed, calories burned, and consistency metrics

### Why Choose Pulse?
- **No Subscriptions**: Free to download and use forever - all features included
- **Privacy First**: Your fitness data stays on your device - 100% private
- **Instant Setup**: Start your first daily workout in seconds - no account needed
- **Motivating Design**: Beautiful interface with satisfying feedback keeps you coming back
- **Cardio & Strength**: Balanced workouts covering cardio exercises, strength training, and flexibility

### Perfect For

- Fitness enthusiasts tracking their cardio and strength progress
- Anyone building a consistent daily exercise habit
- Users seeking a personal trainer experience without the cost
