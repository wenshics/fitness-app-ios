# Pulse App - App Store Submission Guide

## Step-by-Step Instructions for "1.0 Prepare for Submission"

---

## SECTION 1: APP INFORMATION

### 1.1 App Name
**What to enter:** `Pulse`
- This is the name displayed on the App Store

### 1.2 Subtitle
**What to enter:** `Your Daily Exercise Companion`
- Shows under the app name in search results
- Keep it concise (30 characters max)

### 1.3 Primary Category
**What to select:** `Health & Fitness`
- This is the main category for your app

### 1.4 Secondary Category (Optional)
**What to select:** `Lifestyle` (optional)
- Helps with discoverability

### 1.5 Keywords
**What to enter:** 
```
daily workout, fitness tracker, exercise plans, workout timer, fitness goals, home workouts, habit tracker, strength training, cardio exercises, personal trainer
```
- Separate keywords with commas
- Max 100 characters total
- These help users find your app in search

### 1.6 Description
**What to enter:** Use the full description from APP_STORE_DESCRIPTION.md
- Start with: "Pulse is your daily workout companion and fitness tracker..."
- Include all key features and benefits
- Max 4,000 characters

### 1.7 Promotional Text
**What to enter (Optional):**
```
Get AI-powered daily workouts with zero subscriptions!
```
- Shows in featured section (max 170 characters)
- Update this seasonally for promotions

### 1.8 Support URL
**What to enter:** Your support website or email
- Example: `https://fitlifeapp-tckcq6vs.manus.space/support`
- Or: Create a simple support page

### 1.9 Privacy Policy URL
**What to enter:** Your privacy policy URL
- **IMPORTANT**: You MUST have a privacy policy
- Create one at: https://www.privacypolicygenerator.info/
- Or use a simple one: "We don't collect any personal data. All data is stored locally on your device."

### 1.10 Copyright
**What to enter:** 
```
© 2026 Pulse. All rights reserved.
```
- Or: Your company name and year

## SECTION 9: SUBMIT FOR REVIEW

### Final Steps:
1. Review all information one more time
2. Click "Submit for Review" button
3. Confirm submission (you'll get a confirmation email)
4. Wait for Apple's review (typically 24-48 hours)

### What Happens Next:
- **In Review** → Apple is testing your app (24-48 hours)
- **Ready for Sale** → Approved! App goes live
- **Rejected** → Apple will explain why; you can fix and resubmit

---

## COMMON REJECTION REASONS & HOW TO AVOID

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Missing privacy policy | Add privacy policy URL before submission |
| Crashes on startup | Test thoroughly on real device |
| Misleading description | Match description to actual features |
| Poor app quality | Ensure all buttons work and no bugs |
| Requires login but not explained | Clarify that login is optional |
| Incomplete features | Don't promise features not implemented |

---

## AFTER APPROVAL

Once your app is approved and live:
