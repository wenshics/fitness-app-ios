3. ✅ Click **Prepare for Submission**

### 10. WHAT HAPPENS NEXT

**Timeline:**
- **Submission:** Immediately after you click "Add for Review"
- **Processing:** 24-48 hours (Apple reviews your app)
- **Decision:**
