Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a workout?
Open Pulse, view your personalized daily plan, and tap "Start Workout" to begin with guided exercises and timers.
Can I customize my workout?
Yes! With Pulse Premium, you can customize exercise selection, duration, and difficulty level to match your goals.
How does the streak system work?
Complete at least one workout per day to build your streak. Your streak resets if you miss a day, so stay consistent!
What's included in Premium?
Premium unlocks unlimited workouts, advanced customization, priority support, and exclusive achievements.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can manage your subscription in the app under Profile → Subscription, or through your device's app store settings.
