Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Pulse ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: name and email address when you register.
  • Profile data: optional birthday, height, and weight you provide.
  • Subscription data: billing is handled by Apple App Store. We do not store payment details.
  • Usage data: workout logs and achievements stored locally on your device and in your account.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Pulse app experience.
  • To process subscription payments via Apple App Store.
  • To send workout reminders (only with your permission).
  • To send account-related emails (verification, password reset).

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with trusted service providers (Supabase for authentication and data storage, RevenueCat for subscription management, Apple for payment processing) as necessary to operate the app.

Data Retention

You may delete your account at any time by contacting us. We will remove your personal data within 30 days of a verified deletion request.

Children's Privacy

Pulse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
